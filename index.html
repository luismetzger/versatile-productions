Thirty Five Years of Experience

with major television networks and fortune 100-500 companies

Versatile has the knowledge and the edge to produce creative and diverse projects for its clients. To sustain a  business since 1981 Versatile has kept up with technology to the highest degree, as it relates new media integration.   What is “New Media”  Versatile defines it in three areas, software  hardware and the internet.  There is a vast amount of software available to everyone, but knowing what to use for specific application or projects can be the key to how you want your video to look, feel, move.  

Versatile uses professional HD cameras, but everyone has HD cameras on their devices,  so weather we shoot footage or you shoot the footage, the trick is to how you integrate various formats in a project and maintain a professional outcome and Versatile has the experience to take your vision to a presentable professional level.  Oh yes the internet, knowing how to utilize social media during your production is #1 important.

Versatile can optimize your project so it can be distributed and shared with other media partners to move you project goal forward at a high level.  Versatile Productions takes pride in what we do, quality, integrity, and creative fortitude will continue to move our business into the next century.

Versatile Productions  culture is one of creativity and resourcefulness.  Using the tools available within all facets of New Media, we spend our time formulating new ideas directed toward your defined objectives.  The development of creative, targeted and effective communication initiatives are essential to an organization’s success.  VPI works with each client to understand their culture so that we can create a unique program concept.  Your message will be subtly integrated into all elements of the program – from initial branding and logo design to promotional media to web site execution.
