FAQ

Versatile Productions is a full service video production company offering live streaming for TV, film & social media.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We get lots of questions. Here are some answers. If you don’t happen to see what you’re looking for, please contact us with the form above, and we’ll get back to you posthaste.

What is with the name?
As Midwest natives from hardworking, blue-collar families, we wanted a name that expressed our values: honesty, hard work, and trust.

I guess I just don’t understand, do you sell grains?
Nope. We sell purty designs.

Are you hiring?
Yes, checkout the careers page to learn more! I’m a student, will you review my portfolio? We truly value our community, and we love to help students out. However, our time is very limited, so we make it a point to limit the amount of portfolio reviews we do. Each year, we participate in Meet the Pros and several different AIGA Nebraska events.

What industries do you work with?
We love all industries. We’ve worked with a dentist, an insurance company, and a myriad of tech startups. We would rather have clients that align with our design style and core values. We believe a good client relationship starts with an appreciation of each other’s work. We want to work with people who love our design, laugh at our jokes, and enjoy collaboration. However, if you’re a brewery, winery or any other boozery, please call now.

What do you charge?
Our pricing is based on the amount of hours your project will take. In order to determine that, we will need to have a brief discussion about your project, the level of detail involved, technical requirements, etc. The best way to get the ball rolling on that process is to send an email to hello@grainandmortar.com, and we’ll ask you some questions.

I’m from a foreign country, and I give you best SEOs of all time?

You found our website right? We all good.

How long will it take you to complete my project?
Each timeline is different, and is determined by the amount of deliverables and the level of intricacy your project requires. Once we get more information about your project, we’ll be able to give you an estimated timeframe. The best way to get the ball rolling on that process is to send an email to hello@grainandmortar.com, and we’ll ask you some questions.

Where are you located? Where’s Omaha? Do you live on a farm?
Omaha, Nebraska. No, we don’t live in a cornfield. No, we don’t eat steak for every meal. Yes, we love our state and our proud of our Midwestern roots. Also, ya’ll are the ones with the accents, not us.

What is your perfect client?
Our perfect client is someone who loves great design, likes collaboration, and appreciates our creative input. We enjoy working with clients who understand the value of what we do and want to use design to make their businesses more successful.

So, you guys do web development too? How does that work?
Our development skills are something that set us apart. We have a full time web developer on staff, and three of our designers are equally skilled at front-end development. We pay equal attention to development and design. We approach development in one of two ways: 1) We complete everything in-house, or 2) We work in collaboration with your team. By looking at our portfolio, you can see our dedication and commitment to quality, standards compliant HTML / CSS, and responsive design. If you have questions about what it’s like to work with us, just send us a note at hello@grainandmortar.com.

Ready to talk? Just let us know who to contact. That easy :)
